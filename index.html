Dungeon Game

INTRUCTIONS:
  • Movement: WASD keys or arrow keys
  • Enemies: The devil-esque figures on the map when you start the game. If you are adjacent but not diagonally so, you will have to fight the enemy:
  • Walls: They're just that. They're in your way and you have to go around them.
  • Battle: When the battle starts you can't move. You must "skirmish" with the enemy; hit the skirmish button. Right now who attacks is decided at 50% random chance. If you hp is equal to or less than zero you loose and the game ends. If you win the dead enemy is removed from the map. Once all the enemies are off the map you win the game and the game is over
  • Thanks for playing.
